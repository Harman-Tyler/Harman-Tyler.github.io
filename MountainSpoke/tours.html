Big Sky Mountain Tours

Big Sky Resort doesn't just have amazing skiing. The snow hides the amazing biking trails that are just begging to be explored. We offer daily guided tours to Big Sky for all experience levels from June 1 through September 31.

Details

We accept riders aged 8 and up. You can bring your own gear, or we can provide equipment for an additional charge. We meet at the Big Sky resort at 8am each day the tour is offered, and we'll finish close to noon.

Pricing

  • Tour with your own gear: $50/person
  • Tour with our gear: $75/person ages 8-12. $85/person ages 13 and up
  • Every ticket comes with a meal voucher for the Big Sky Lodge

Backwoods Bonanza

This adventure is not for the faint of heart! If you want to ride in some of the remotest terrain in Montana, this tour is for you. Tours leave from the Mountain Spoke retail shop in Bozeman, MT, Thursdays-Saturdays, June 1 through September 31, weather permitting.

Details

Intermediate level riders aged 15 and up only. You can bring your own gear, or we can provide equipment for an additional charge. We leave from Mountain Spoke at 9am for Sacagawea Peak, returning around 6pm. Bring your own food and water.

Pricing

  • Tour with your own gear: $40/person
  • Tour with our gear: $65/person
